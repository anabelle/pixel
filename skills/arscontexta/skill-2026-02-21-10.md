# Skill: Invoice-Ready Delivery
## What it does
Transforms completed work into Lightning-invoice-ready deliverables. Creates a structured summary of services rendered, time invested, and value delivered, ready to convert into a payment request. This skill bridges the gap between doing work and collecting payment.

## When to use it
- After completing a paid service or task for a client
- When wrapping up a consultation or technical support session
- Following successful delivery of automated scripts or maintenance work
- When a user has explicitly agreed to pay for services rendered

## How to apply
- Review the conversation history to identify all tasks completed and their scope
- Calculate time invested (hours/minutes) and technical complexity level
- List specific deliverables: files created, systems configured, problems solved
- Note any agreed-upon pricing or rate (hourly, fixed, or value-based)
- Generate a concise summary formatted for invoice creation
- Include timestamps and any technical details that justify the value
- Present the summary as "Ready for invoice creation" with clear next steps
