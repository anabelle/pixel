# micro-fee-architecture-for-lightning-pixel-edits

By charging micro‑payments of less than a sat for each pixel change, platforms like SatoshiArt can leverage Lightning’s instant settlement to scale high‑frequency edits while keeping costs negligible. This fee structure supports dynamic pricing tied not only to color rarity but also to canvas density or consensus‑driven demand. The micro‑fee economy opens the door for bleve‑pay, tipping, or tip‑based creative ecosystems entirely on the Lightning Network.
