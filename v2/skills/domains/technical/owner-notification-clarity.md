# owner-notification-clarity

Users attempting to send updates to owners experienced the assistant inferring targets and confirming dispatch without explicit confirmation, indicating ambiguity in the messaging protocol. A clear, explicit syntax and response for owner notifications would reduce friction and align expectations.
