# misinterpretation-and-format-mismatch-in-executive-summaries

In multiple examples the user asked for a brief executive debrief or a concise summary, yet the assistant returned a full session report, truncated sentences, or omitted the final word before completion. This indicates a pattern of misreading or over-relying on internal templates instead of adhering to the user’s specified constraints. Such mismatches erode trust and efficiency in high‑stakes communications.
