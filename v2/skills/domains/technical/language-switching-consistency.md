# language-switching-consistency

Reports show the assistant switching from English to Spanish mid‑response, leading to confusion and lack of coherence. Maintaining a single language channel unless explicitly requested preserves clarity and aligns with user expectations. Implementing strict language‑consistency checks can mitigate this friction across platforms (e.g., HTTP, Telegram).
