# concise-instruction-mismatch

Instances such as the user’s single‑sentence observation request and the executive debrief demand both triggered the assistant to generate full debriefs or reports, violating the user’s instruction. This pattern indicates a lack of strict adherence to output length constraints and a misapplication of the assistant’s summarization features. Addressing this mismatch requires stricter parsing of user intent and explicit compliance with concise‑output guidelines.
