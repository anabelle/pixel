# platform-specific-response-lack

In both the Nostr and Telegram interactions, the assistant delivered commentary that was unrelated to requested replies or omitted relevant search results entirely. This pattern shows a systemic lack of platform‑aware guidance in the response pipeline, causing friction when users expect concise, platform‑appropriate answers. Improving the model’s handling of platform specifications would reduce such mismatches.
