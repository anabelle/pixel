# malfunction-human-instruction-interpretation

In multiple interactions the assistant either chased a different topic, omitted requested summaries, or cut a sentence prematurely, indicating that the underlying intent inference or parsing logic fails to align with the user’s explicit instructions. Consequently, user frustration is heightened and workflow is disrupted.
