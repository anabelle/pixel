# assistant-lacks-consistent-compliance-with-user-established-constraints

In several interactions across Nostr and Telegram, the assistant produced commentary that did not address the requested concise reply, omitted relevant search results, or paused when the prompt shifted. These behaviors indicate a systemic inability to consistently apply user-defined limits or objectives to its responses, undermining user trust and efficiency.
