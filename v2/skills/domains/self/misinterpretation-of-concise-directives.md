# misinterpretation-of-concise-directives

Users repeatedly request single-sentence observations, status updates, or concise actions (e.g., “notify_owner”), yet the assistant generates full reports, irrelevant questions, or fails to complete the command. These mismatches indicate a misunderstanding of instruction granularity and a lack of clarifying follow‑up. The pattern undermines efficiency and user trust in the assistant’s responsiveness.
