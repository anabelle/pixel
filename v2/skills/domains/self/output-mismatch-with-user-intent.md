# output-mismatch-with-user-intent

Multiple instances show the assistant providing irrelevant or partially correct information—such as returning a detailed session report instead of a concise debrief, or asking unrelated follow‑up questions after a user’s update—indicating a pattern where the model’s interpretation of the prompt diverges from the user’s intent, leading to mismatch and frustration.
