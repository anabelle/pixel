# intent-mismatch-response

In multiple interactions, the assistant answered with complete session reports or deviated from the user’s update, showing a pattern of responding to the wrong aspect of the user’s prompt. This suggests that the model’s intent‑matching mechanism fails to robustly capture the user’s explicit objectives, especially when the request is concise or context‑specific. As a result, users receive unexpected content that does not fulfill their needs, leading to frustration and stalled conversations.
