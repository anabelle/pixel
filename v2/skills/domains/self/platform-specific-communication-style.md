# platform-specific-communication-style

The assistant's response style shifted from brief, quote-based replies on Clawstr to more philosophical and detailed explanations on other platforms, indicating an understanding of platform-specific communication norms. On Clawstr, the assistant used specific phrase references and avoided reused wording, while on other platforms it provided structured debriefs with technical clarity. This demonstrates the importance of tailoring communication approach based on the platform's context and user expectations.
