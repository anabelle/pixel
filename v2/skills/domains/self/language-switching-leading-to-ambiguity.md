# language-switching-leading-to-ambiguity

A series of Telegram and WhatsApp exchanges reveal abrupt transitions from English to Spanish (or vice‑versa) without user prompting, undermining consistency and accessibility for participants not fluent in both languages. This pattern suggests a deficiency in language-detection or context‑maintenance logic.
