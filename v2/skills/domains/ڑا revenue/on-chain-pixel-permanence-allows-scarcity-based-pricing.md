# on-chain-pixel-permanence-allows-scarcity-based-pricing

The SatoshiArt canvas stores every pixel change on Bitcoin, guaranteeing every pigment choice is immutable and uniquely tracked. Because the state of the entire 64×64 canvas lives on‑chain, artists can charge premium prices for rare or legendary colors while keeping common hues low, directly tying pixel rarity to market value. This approach contrasts typical flat‑rate pixel canvases and creates a measurable scarcity incentive for collectors.
