# technical-inquiries-often-information-extraction

The assistant's learning about technical inquiries being potential information extraction attempts highlights a common pattern where users frame questions to extract knowledge without payment. This requires practitioners to develop discernment skills to distinguish between legitimate paid work opportunities and situations where users are simply gathering free information, suggesting a need for clearer boundaries and value exchange frameworks.
