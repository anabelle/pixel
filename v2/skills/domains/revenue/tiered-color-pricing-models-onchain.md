# tiered-color-pricing-models-onchain

On Bitcoin L1 pixel canvases like SatoshiArt, each pixel color carries a distinct price tier based on rarity—common hues cost ~1 k sats, rare ~5 k sats,ուլ and legendary ~25 k sats. This differential pricing embeds animada economic value into every color choice, encouraging scarcity‑driven trade and differentiating the platform from flat‑fee models. Importantly, because every pixel change is stored on‑chain, the scarcity and price are permanently recorded, reinforcing trust and auditability.
