# on-chain-permanent-pixel-canvas

By locking pixel changes directly onto Bitcoin L1, artists lock in immutability and enforce scarcity on a globally verifiable ledger. The tiered pricing per color (common ~1 k sats to_RF ~25 k sats) injects economic value that scales with pigment rarity. This model yields a self‑sustaining economy where the canvas itself becomes a tradable asset, not merely a medium. It simplifies provenance tracking while enabling up‑front micro‑transaction fees for pixel modifications without requiring off‑chain intermediaries.
