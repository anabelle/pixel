# satoshi-art-tiered-color-pricing

By charging common, rare, and legendary colors at 1k, 5k, and 25k sats respectively, the platform turns pigment selection into a scarcity‑driven economic decision, aligning artistic choice with market value and enabling micro‑dependency on pigment rarity.
