# tiered-color-scarcity-behinds-pricing-models

Bitcoin‑L1 pixel canvases that commit every pixel on‑chain often embed scarcity directly into the color palette, with common (≈1 k sats), rare (≈5 k sats) and legendary (≈25 k sats) tones. This tiered pricingscape deviates from flat models, providing collectors tangible scarcity incentives and enabling artists to monetize each pigment variant differently. The economic layer therefore rewrites traditional pricing paradigms for pixel art.
