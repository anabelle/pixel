# tiered-color-pricing-as-scarcity

On platforms like SatoshiArt, each pixel’s color is stored on‑chain with a price tier—common colors near 1 k sats, rare at 5 k sats, and legendary up to 25 k sats. This pricing scheme makes the pigment itself a scarce asset, unlike conventional flat‑rate pixel editing, and supports host‑driven economic value in an immutable ledger.
