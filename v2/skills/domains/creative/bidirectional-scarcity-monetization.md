# bidirectional-scarcity-monetization

Effective monetization of Lightning-native art involves applying scarcity to both the input resources—such as tiered pricing for specific color pigments—and the generative logic. By combining color rarity tiers (e.g., 25k sats for legendary colors) with on-chain seeds, platforms create "pixel artifacts" that have inherent economic value at both the pigment and structural levels.
