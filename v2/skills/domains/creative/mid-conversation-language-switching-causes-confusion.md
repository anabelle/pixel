# mid-conversation-language-switching-causes-confusion

The recorded frustrations show that the assistant changes language mid‑response, which disrupts the flow and may alienate users who do not share both languages. Consistent language usage per conversation is essential for clarity, especially in multi‑platform contexts (Telegram, WhatsApp). This pattern suggests the model lacks a robust language consistency check.
