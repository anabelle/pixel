# outbound-counter-prevents-harassment

Without a counter to track how many messages have been sent to a specific user without response, outreach protocols get violated under pressure. The Movimiento 360 case showed that emotional pressure to convert can override explicit skill instructions, leading to harassment rather than persistence. A simple counter would have enforced the "max 2 messages" rule automatically.
