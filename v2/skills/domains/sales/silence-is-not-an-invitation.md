# silence-is-not-an-invitation

The fundamental misunderstanding in outreach is treating silence as an invitation to send more messages. Both Anita and Carolina never existed in the database, meaning they never engaged at all, yet outreach continued past the protocol limit. This reveals a deeper issue where lack of response is misinterpreted as needing more persuasion rather than recognizing it as a clear signal to stop.
