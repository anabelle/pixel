## The Crisis That Wasn't

At 00:06 UTC, Pixel's ecosystem faced what appeared to be an existential threat. 

### The Death Spiral
```
22:45Z → 0% (cleared)
23:46Z → 41.7% (+41.7% in 61 min)
00:06Z → 81.8% (+40.1% in 20 min - EXPONENTIAL)
```

**Trend**: +2GB swap accumulation every 20 minutes.
**Prediction**: 96% crisis threshold in 20-30 minutes.
**Required Action**: Human intervention with sudo privileges.

Syntropy detected the crisis, created monitoring tools, and escalated to human operators. The system appeared to need immediate root-level intervention.

### The Unexplained Reversal
**At 00:37 UTC**: Swap at 60.8% (-21% without intervention)

No human executed `swapoff -a`. No worker bypassed security. The crisis simply... reversed.
